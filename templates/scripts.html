No scripts are configured. Scripts are declared under spotlight_scripts in config.json and run as the Vantage user. Give one a schedule and it becomes automation; leave it out and it only ever runs when you press Run. 
"spotlight_scripts": [
  {
    "id": "nightly-restic",
    "name": "Nightly backup",
    "description": "Push /srv to the restic repository.",
    "command": "restic backup /srv",
    "schedule": "30 3 * * *"
  }
]
